Workshop on bubbling phenomena in geometric analysis

Zürich, Wednesday the 4th of March 2026

This workshop aims at gathering young researchers working on bubbling analysis in the broad sense. The objective is to share problems and techniques related to this topic, fostering new collaborations. To this aim, talks will be offered featuring various aspects and applications of bubbling in different fields of geometric analysis, to emphasize the usefulness and ubiquity of this concept and allow the exchange of ideas between members of various communities in geometric analysis.
This workshop is intended as a convivial event between young researchers sharing common mathematical interests, and a special emphasis will be put on the pedagogy of the lectures that will be given, that should be accessible to non-experts and with the amount of prerequisites kept to a minimum.

All the practical information regarding this event is gathered on this webpage, and shall be uploaded and kept up to date to serve as a communication support with all interested participants.
