My research interest in short

I am working in the field of geometric analysis. Until now, my research has mainly been focusing on the study of Sobolev mappings with values into manifolds. I especially study questions related to the density of smooth maps among such mappings: strong and weak density problems, detecting topological singularities using analytical objects as the Jacobian, computing the relaxed energy, and so on. However, I am also interested in other questions linked to Sobolev spaces into manifolds, such as extension, lifting, or the study of harmonic maps.
