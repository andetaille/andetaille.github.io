Since the beginning of my PhD, I participate from time to time to the activities of the MMI as a volunteer.

The MMI is a mediation center for mathematics and computer sciences founded by the LABEX MILYON, that offers activities to discover concepts of mathematics and computer sciences in a simple, playful, and relaxed way, intended for students, teachers, and large audience.

During my PhD, I participate from time to time in the Discrete Mathematics Club of Lyon.

The Discrete Mathematics Club organizes sessions on Sunday, or during the week in school holidays periods, to offer challenges to students enthusiast of mathematical problems, and to give them statements and tools to go further.

Such clubs exist in many other cities, for instance in Marseille. Do not hesitate to inform yourselves about those that may exist close to you!

In 2024, I participated in the fifth edition of ComSciCon France.

ComSciCon France is a three days workshop about scientific communication for French-speaking PhD students. The goal is to train PhD students to scientific communication, via practical activities and meetings with working professionnals.

In 2024, I participated in the Maths en Jeans convention in Lyon, by helping with logistics, by participating in the students-researchers meeting, and by chairing a presentations session.

The Maths en Jeans method is the opportunity to initiate high school students to research, by making them work on original problems under the supervision of their teacher and a researcher. The students may then present their results to the other teams during the annual convention.

If you are Belgian like me, please note that such an action also exists in Belgium!
