About me

I am currently (since September 2025) a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zürich, under the supervision of Tristan Rivière.

I prepared my PhD thesis from 2022 to 2025 at Institut Camille Jordan, in Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 (France), under the supervision of Petru Mironescu.
During my third year, from September to December, I was on a long-term research stay at CUNEF Universidad (Madrid), hosted by Óscar Domínguez.

Before that, I studied at the École de Mathématiques of Université catholique de Louvain (Belgium), where I obtained a bachelor and master degree in Mathematical Sciences.
During my second year of master, I did an ERASMUS exchange stay at Université Lyon 1, where I followed the advanced mathematics master (track EDP) jointly organized with the ENS de Lyon.
